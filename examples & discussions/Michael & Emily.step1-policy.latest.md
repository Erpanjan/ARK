## Policy Title
Carter Family Unified Investment and Goal Funding Policy

## Executive Summary
This policy establishes a unified investment and funding strategy for the Carter family, addressing diversification, dedicated education savings, and retirement security. The recommended portfolio deploys $675,000 across global equities, Bitcoin, and cash, with a passive management approach. The plan aligns with the Carters' goals of funding Noah's college education and achieving retirement at age 62, while managing concentration risk and maintaining liquidity for future needs.

## Sections
### Client Background

Michael (36) and Emily Carter (34) reside in Austin, Texas, with their one-year-old son, Noah. Michael is a Senior Operations Manager and Emily is a part-time pediatric nurse. They are a dual-parent, married household focused on education, home stability, and retirement security.

### Client Financial Snapshot

Annual gross income is $242,000. Net monthly take-home ranges from $12,300 to $13,100. Annual spending is $146,400. Assets include $78,000 in bank savings, $275,000 in taxable brokerage (all US equity), and $322,000 in 401(k) (all US treasuries). Mortgage balance is $472,000 at 3.375%.

### Client Financial Needs

Primary needs are diversification of brokerage assets, establishing dedicated education savings for Noah, reviewing insurance coverage, maintaining a 6-month emergency reserve, and monitoring ongoing liabilities such as student loans and childcare expenses.

### Client Investment Preferences and Behavioral Considerations

The Carters prioritize risk reduction, tax efficiency, and goal-based investing. They wish to avoid over-concentration in US equities and value liquidity for future expenses. Behavioral focus is on disciplined, passive investing and maintaining flexibility.

### Taxes, Exclusions, and Exemptions

Current education savings are in a taxable brokerage, not a 529 plan, impacting tax efficiency. No explicit tax exclusions or exemptions are documented. Tax management will be considered in future account structuring.

### Other Special Requirements

No special requirements beyond goal funding, risk management, and maintaining liquidity. Insurance needs are unaddressed and should be reviewed.

### Capital Deployment Timeline

A total of $675,000 USD will be deployed immediately across the recommended portfolio, aligning with current asset balances and goal timelines.

### Portfolio Policy

The unified portfolio will be passively managed, globally diversified, and structured to support both education and retirement goals. Asset allocation reduces concentration risk and maintains sufficient liquidity.
